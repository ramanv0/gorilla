Since the initial release of Gorilla LLM in May, we're incredibly grateful for your overwhelming response. The Gorilla project is loved by developers worldwide. Through our integrations with an ever-growing set of API calls, we've served ~500k requests, all from our small node at UC Berkeley. Some of you have gone ahead to train your own Gorilla models for your custom use-cases, and we love to see the community growing. To enhance the support for individual developers, and enterprises looking to adopt or train their own Gorilla models, we're collaborating with our users and colleagues to launch a comprehensive blog series. In this series, we will delve deeper into the intricacies of Gorilla and outline Berkeley's vision and roadmap for the future of LLMs interacting with the world.
